Understanding Pagefind Hub

Pagefind Hub is a powerful tool designed to aggregate search records from various sources into a single Pagefind index. While Pagefind is excellent at indexing static files, Pagefind Hub extends this capability to external providers.

Key Features

  • Multi-source Indexing: Seamlessly combine local HTML files with records from external platforms.
  • Extensible Providers: Built-in support for Bluesky and YouTube, with the ability to create custom providers.
  • Unified Search Experience: Provide your users with a comprehensive search interface that covers your entire digital footprint.

How it Works

Pagefind Hub fetches data from your configured providers, transforms them into Pagefind-compatible records, and merges them with your local site directory during the indexing process.
